Who we are?

A group of students with multicultural background working towards enhancing inclusion, community values, and academic success.

About Us

What we stand for:
The Improvement of foreign students' knowledge of American culture and their
experience at Oakland Community College. The importance of hosting a welcoming environment
and the success of students through adding value to our community by helping each other.

Our Mission

To enlighten and engage intercultural students in our community through our social activities, such as volunteering, networking , and research practices to develop a successful career as students and professionals.

Our Focus
  1. Academic Success: Sharing our experience in learning and adjusting to the American education system.
  2. Career Development:
    Getting involved in college activities, promoting networking, and researching on our career interests.
  3. Cultural Integration: Volunteering and promoting intercultural inclusion.
