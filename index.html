Who is this for?

Non-technical entrepreneurs and professionals interested in learning more about mobile app development.
For non-tech entrepreneurs, hiring developers to build their app can be prohibitely expesinve, so why not D.I.Y. ?
For non-tech professionals, learning how to build mobile apps will increase their career options.

About me

What I stand for:
I believe that learning about the latest technologies can help non-technical leaders achieve their professional dreams.

What I do for a living:
I've been building apps since 2014. Initially, I got paid when I won prize money from competitions.
Eventually I landed a job as an Android Software Engineer at ASI, a small consultancy in Ann Arbor, MI,
where I spent a year immersed in code, and hosted this consulting event every other Saturday.
As of today, I am back to school and pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science from Lawrence Tech University.

My mission

To help my community get started building mobile apps.

My focus
  • Share my professional experience building mobile apps.
  • Show non-technical entrepreneurs how to get started in a highly technological market.
